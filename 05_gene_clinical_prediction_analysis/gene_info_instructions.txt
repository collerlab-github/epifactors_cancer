Cbioportal

Go to cbioportal.org
Under the Query tab on top left, select the study based on tissue and study (ex: Prostate, TCGA Cell 2015).
Then click 'Explore Selected Studies' on the bottom of the page.
The study id is in the URL after 'id=' (ex 'prad_tcga_pub').
Use this id for the patients in the following scripts.

BRCA - brca_tcga_pub
PRAD - prad_tcga_pub
LUAD - luad_tcga_pub
CRC - coadread_tcga_pub
